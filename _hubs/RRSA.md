By fostering a holistic approach to development, the Reliable Refugee Storytellers Association aims to create resilient, knowledgeable and economically vibrant communities that live harmoniously and sustainably.

RRSA delivers training in digital storytelling and journalism, creates safe platforms for refugee-led narratives, supports peer mentorship and conducts community outreach and awareness campaigns. Through partnerships with local stakeholders, civil society and policymakers, RRSA amplifies refugee voices, promotes social inclusion and dignity, builds livelihood opportunities and influences positive policy and public attitudes toward refugees.
