GoGirls ICT Initiative is a non-profit based in Juba, South Sudan, founded by a group of dedicated young women specializing in Computer Science, Information Systems, ICT4D Innovation, hacktivism, and peacebuilding.

Its focus is on mentorship in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics) topics, utilizing open-source resources to foster an Open Culture in South Sudan, and providing training and workshops in this field. Due to its consistent advocacy for digital inclusion, GoGirls ICT Initiative is a stakeholder in the development of national frameworks, strategies, and policies in South Sudan.

Established in 2015, the organization was officially registered as an independent Non-Governmental Organization in 2019.

The organization is part of the Global Innovation Gathering and the Access to Skills and Knowledge network (#ASKnet), while its founders have been featured on E-Skills4Girls' "The Storytellers: Experiences Around the Globe."

For more information, please visit our website at https://gogirlsict.org/ or email us at info@gogirlsict.org.
