Junub Open Space (JOS) is a community-based educational hub dedicated to peacebuilding among youth through knowledge sharing and access to skill-enhancing facilities. JOS provides a platform for exploring creativity, innovation, and talent, empowering youth and children in South Sudan to become resilient and self-reliant. Founded in 2016 and legally registered in March 2017, JOS supports youth empowerment and fosters a culture of innovation across South Sudan.

JOS is part of several regional and international networks, such as Together for Inclusion, #ASKnet, Afrilabs, I4Policy Foundation, and the Global Innovation Gathering, strengthening collaboration and innovation across borders.
